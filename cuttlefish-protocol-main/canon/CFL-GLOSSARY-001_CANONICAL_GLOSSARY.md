# Cuttlefish Labs — Canonical Glossary of Terms

**Document ID:** CFL-GLOSSARY-001
**Status:** Draft v1.0 (Proposed Canonical Reference)
**Authority:** Navigator (David Hans Elze)
**Purpose:** Establish a single, authoritative glossary for all canonical terminology used throughout the Cuttlefish Labs ecosystem. Every AI agent, developer, writer, designer, attorney, and collaborator should reference this glossary before introducing new terminology.

---

## A

### Activity Registry
The append-only canonical event log that records every significant action performed within the CAC Protocol. Every participation reward, Stewardship action, KYA event, and governance decision must trace to an Activity Registry event.

### Agent
A registered autonomous or semi-autonomous AI actor operating within the Cuttlefish ecosystem under a documented Principal Chain. Agents are registered actors, not legal persons.

### Anchor
The highest CAC Membership Tier. "Anchor" may also describe blockchain anchoring operations in technical documentation. Context determines meaning.

---

## B

### Builder Agent
A name-locked investor persona. This is not a membership tier and must never be renamed because the historical "Builder Tier" was retired.

### Builder Steward
The canonical actor type responsible for engineering review, governance participation, technical attestation, and infrastructure stewardship.

Builder Stewards replace the crypto terms:
- validator
- staker
- node operator

Builder Stewards earn authority through Stewardship Standing rather than capital.

---

## C

### CAC
**Compute Access Certificate**

The prepaid compute credential that grants access to AI inference, participation, governance, and Earth 2.0 services.

Never expand CAC as:
- Constitutional Agent License
- Constitutional Audit Card
- any other variation

### CAC Card
The physical or digital credential associated with a Compute Access Certificate.

Functions:
- Identity
- Compute access
- Membership

Not:
- savings account
- investment product
- yield product

### CAC Protocol
The complete governance, identity, participation, compute, and reward framework that powers the Cuttlefish ecosystem.

### Constitutional Stack
The identity framework consisting of:
- CONSTITUTION.md
- SOUL.md

Defines behavioral and governance rules for AI agents.

### Contributor
The second Stewardship level.

Contributors participate in lower-risk review activities while building Stewardship Standing.

### Council Steward
The highest Builder Steward governance role.

Council Stewards participate in:
- constitutional governance
- appeals
- framework evolution
- dispute review

## K

### Know Your Agent (KYA)
The identity verification framework for autonomous AI participants.

KYA establishes:
- Principal identity
- Authority chain
- Credential verification
- Participation rights

KYA is conceptually similar to KYC but applies to autonomous agents.

## P

### Participant
Entry-level Builder Steward role.

Observation, learning, and limited participation.

### Principal
The legally recognized human or organization responsible for an Agent.

Every registered Agent must terminate at a legally recognized Principal.

### Principal Chain
The documented authority hierarchy linking an Agent to its legal Principal.

Maintained through KYA.

### Proposal-to-Reward Pipeline (PRP)
The five-layer workflow connecting:

Proposal → Review → Activity → Governance → Participation Reward

## R

### Reward Router
The system responsible for routing participation rewards to the appropriate legal distribution rail.

## S

### Senior Steward
Experienced Builder Steward with authority to:
- review Builder Stewards
- participate in arbitration
- mentor Contributors

### SOUL.md
The canonical identity document for an individual AI agent.

Defines:
- personality
- values
- mission
- behavioral constraints

### Standing
Short form of Stewardship Standing.

### Stewardship Ladder
The progression system:

Participant → Contributor → Builder Steward → Senior Steward → Council Steward

### Stewardship Standing
The earned, domain-specific measure of competence, trust, and participation.

Standing:
- cannot be purchased
- cannot be transferred
- cannot be staked
- is earned over time

Standing is the primary bond of Builder Stewards.

### Studio
The third CAC Membership Tier.

## Glossary Governance Rule

This glossary is a living constitutional reference. New terms may be proposed by collaborators or AI agents, but they become canonical only after explicit approval by the Navigator. Once approved and entered into this glossary, a term is considered name-locked and may not be renamed, reinterpreted, or repurposed without a ratified Navigator decision. Historical terms should remain documented to preserve continuity and auditability. This glossary should be treated as the single source of truth for terminology across software, specifications, legal documents, governance materials, and public communications.
