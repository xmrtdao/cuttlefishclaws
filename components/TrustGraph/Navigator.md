# Navigator

**Role:** Human Authority  
**Type:** Governance  
**Status:** Sovereign

The Navigator is the human authority at the top of the constitutional hierarchy. All agent actions flow from Navigator commands. Holds structural override authority over every agent in the system.

## Constitutional Authority

- Issues **licenses** to [[CAC]] (Compute Access Credential)
- Issues **commands** to [[Agent]] (Constitutional AI)
- Receives **governance** from [[DAO Gov]]
- Final override on all constitutional matters

## Trust Architecture

The Navigator does not carry a TrustGraph score — trust is inherent to human authority. The Navigator can:

- Veto any governance proposal via [[FounderShare.sol]]
- Override agent decisions
- Appoint and remove governance agents
